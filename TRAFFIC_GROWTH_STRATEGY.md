# Traffic Growth Strategy for Royal Mabati

## 1. SEO Optimization (Organic Traffic)

### On-Page SEO
- [ ] Add meta descriptions to all pages
- [ ] Optimize heading structure (H1, H2, H3)
- [ ] Target keywords: "roofing sheets Kenya", "corrugated roofing", "tile profile sheets", etc.
- [ ] Add internal links between related content
- [ ] Optimize image alt text for SEO

### Technical SEO
- [ ] Generate and submit sitemap.xml to Google Search Console
- [ ] Create robots.txt (already exists)
- [ ] Enable Core Web Vitals monitoring
- [ ] Set up canonical URLs
- [ ] Fix any crawl errors in Google Search Console

### Content Strategy
- [ ] Blog posts on roofing topics (3-4 per month)
  - "Best Roofing Sheets for Kenya's Climate"
  - "How to Choose Between Box Profile and Corrugated"
  - "Maintenance Tips for Long-lasting Roofing"
  - "Roofing Installation Guide"

### Local SEO
- [ ] Add Google Business Profile (if not done)
- [ ] Ensure consistent name/address/phone across listings
- [ ] Encourage customer reviews
- [ ] Add local schema markup

## 4. Social Media Strategy

### Content Pillars
1. **Educational** (40%) - Roofing tips, installation guides
2. **Promotional** (30%) - Product showcases, offers
3. **Social Proof** (20%) - Customer testimonials, project photos
4. **Engagement** (10%) - Polls, Q&A, conversations

### Platforms
- **Facebook**: Community building, customer service
  - Post 3-5x per week
  - Engage with followers daily
  - Join relevant groups and share expertise
  
- **Instagram**: Visual storytelling
  - Post 3-4x per week
  - Use local hashtags (#RoofingKenya, #MabatiSheets)
  - Stories for behind-the-scenes
  - Reels for product demos

- **WhatsApp Business**: Direct customer engagement
  - Broadcast updates
  - Quick responses to inquiries
  - Share product catalogs

- **LinkedIn**: B2B outreach
  - Share industry insights
  - Connect with contractors
  - Post 2x per week

- **TikTok**: Reach younger demographic
  - Quick roofing installation clips
  - Before/after transformations
  - Educational shorts

## Tips for Success

✅ **Be consistent** - Regular posting and engagement matter more than volume
✅ **Focus on quality** - Better to have 100 engaged followers than 10,000 inactive ones
✅ **Listen to customers** - Engage with comments and questions
✅ **Be authentic** - Share real stories and real results
✅ **Data-driven** - Make decisions based on analytics, not hunches
✅ **Local focus** - Emphasize Kenya-specific benefits and success stories
✅ **Mobile-first** - Most traffic comes from phones
✅ **Test & iterate** - What works changes; always be testing
